About Us

Vibz Travel is an experienced travel management company. We work with you to manage all elements of your travel in an efficient, cost effective and ethical manner. The company is evolving into a highly respected purpose driven travel management company. Vibz Travel is committed to making a difference in the world that its clients travel to, whether on business, on holiday or on a study tour. With offices all over India, the company is focused on managing the travel for companies as well as individual holidays.

Headquartered in the Indian Capital, Delhi and having a strong market presence already, Vibz travels is creating a niche for itself with its Global market having an extensive sales strength which makes it seamless for us to connect to our Travel Partners and making it a world wide organisation.

We aim to increase profit levels marginally and provide a stress free Travel services to our Customers.

“To be the most trusted and respected Travel Agency recognized by our clients for delivering excellence, to be a leading provider of Travel Solution across India. And to continuously create new opportunities for growth in our strategic business”.
